## What to review weekly

- Which post got the most bot-related replies?
- Which CTA generated the most workflow requests?
- What bot use case appears most: lead capture, FAQ, appointment, affiliate, support, or follow-up?
- Are people confused by bot language?
- Does the website section need more examples?

## Improvement prompt

```text
Review this Chatlippytm.ai.Bots performance log and suggest improvements to the bot workflow card, CTA, website section, social posts, intake questions, and follow-up offer. Goal: increase qualified bot workflow requests.
```

## Best practices

- Track questions people ask repeatedly.
- Turn repeated questions into bot FAQ content.
- Turn strong replies into service offer language.
- Improve the CTA if people are interested but not clicking.
