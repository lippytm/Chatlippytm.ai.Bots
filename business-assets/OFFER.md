# Chatlippytm.ai.Bots Offer Path

## Core offer position

`Chatlippytm.ai.Bots` helps businesses map practical AI/chatbot workflows for lead capture, FAQs, qualification, routing, follow-up, and human handoff.

## Free offer

**Bot Workflow Starter Checklist**

A simple checklist to identify the first useful bot workflow.

## Lead magnet

**Build a Bot Workflow Worksheet**

Helps map:

- Lead source.
- First question.
- FAQ answers.
- Qualification questions.
- Offer routing.
- Follow-up message.
- Human handoff.

## Service offers

### Bot Workflow Map

Create a clear conversation map before building the bot.

### ManyChat / BotBuilders Setup Plan

Turn the bot workflow map into an implementation plan.

### AI Follow-Up Flow

Create follow-up messages and routing logic for new leads.

### Monthly Bot Optimization

Review bot performance, improve questions, tighten CTA paths, and track lead quality.

## CTA ladder

1. Build a Bot Workflow.
2. Complete the Bot Workflow Worksheet.
3. Request a Bot Flow Map.
4. Ask about ManyChat/BotBuilders implementation.

## Compliance notes

Bots should support business workflows, not replace all judgment or customer service. Include human handoff and avoid unsupported claims about guaranteed leads, sales, or income.
